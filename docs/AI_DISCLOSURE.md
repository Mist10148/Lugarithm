# Lugarithm AI Usage Disclosure

This is the running disclosure log for AI used during development of *Lugarithm* and inside the
shipping game. Append entries when a new AI tool, model, generated asset, runtime integration, or
materially AI-assisted workflow is introduced. Do not remove old entries; add corrections as new
dated rows.

*Lugarithm* is a submission to **AI Game On! IV · AI Fest 2026** (Region VI – Western Visayas) by
**Team Cyfer**. AI is used in two distinct ways, both disclosed below: (1) **development assistance**
— tools that helped the team write code and produce assets; and (2) **runtime AI** — live, in-game
systems the player interacts with, powered by Google Gemini.

## Disclosure Rules

- Record the tool/model, date, area, concrete use, human review, and shipped output.
- Distinguish **development assistance** from **runtime AI** visible to players.
- Record generated art, music, voices, dialogue, lore, code, and research separately.
- Historical claims require verification against credible sources; AI output is never a source of
  fact. Heritage facts are authored from sourced research (`docs/HERITAGE_RESEARCH.md`); runtime AI
  only rephrases authored, fact-checked text and is forbidden from inventing history.
- Runtime AI keys live only in the local, git-ignored root `.env` (synced to a generated
  `Assets/Resources/ai_config.json`); no secret is committed to the repository.
- Folklore is always labelled as folklore, never presented as archaeological fact.
